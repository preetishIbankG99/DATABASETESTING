======================
Alter table<table Name>

========================
Alter table<table Name>
ADD/DROP CONSTRAINT<constraint name>NULL/NOT NULL;
3)TO ENABLE/DISABLE CONSTRAINT
==============================
Alter table<table Name>
ENABLE/DISABLE CONSTRAINT<constraint Name>;
